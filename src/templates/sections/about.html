Meet the Author

Joshua Newton

Soul Writer

A former journalist and a thourough skeptic, Josh
once disliked the idea of ‘Soul’m ‘God’, etc.
It took him many years of suffering, to make him kneel
before the silent, glowing truth hudden within.
And once the iner journey began, his reversal of mind was steady and quick.
Leaving a succesful twin career in media and screenwriting,
Josh took to quiet life of contemplation and depth writing from the soul.
Without any formal jobs or earnings, he began to share
on social media, his learnings with
whoever came asking.
He calls himself ‘a half monk and a half monkey’ and
continues to serve the needy with advaitic wisdom.

Joshua Netwon draws his inspiration mostly from the teachings of Ramana Maharishi, A Course in Miracles, and his own inner Self.
