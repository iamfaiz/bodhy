Meet the Author

Joshua Newton

Soul Writer

A former journalist and a thorough skeptic,
Josh once disliked the concepts of ‘soul, ‘god’ etc.
It took him many years of suffering to make him kneel
before the silent, glowing truth hidden within.
And once the inner journey began,
his reversal of mind was steady and quick.
Leaving behind a successful twin career in media and screenwriting,
Josh took to a quiet life of contemplation and depth writing from the soul.
Without any formal jobs or earnings, he began to share on social media,
his learnings with whoever came asking.
He calls himself ‘half a monk, half a monkey’
and continues to serve the needy with non-dual wisdom.

Joshua Netwon draws his inspiration mostly from the teachings of Ramana Maharishi, A Course in Miracles, and his own inner Self.
